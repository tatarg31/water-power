What is Water Power?

Water Power is a smart, modular water filtration system designed to purify contaminated water while ensuring responsible waste handling.

How It Works

Water Power uses sensor‑guided modular filtration. Only the necessary stages activate, reducing energy use and extending cartridge life.

Filter Modules

Pre‑Filter Cartridge
Captures sediment, insects, and visible debris to protect downstream modules.
Biological Filter
Uses immobilised, non‑pathogenic bacteria to break down organic contaminants.
Activated Carbon Cartridge
Adsorbs chlorine, odours, pesticides, and heavy metals.
Reverse Osmosis Membrane
Removes salts, microplastics, dissolved chemicals, and radionuclides.
pH & Mineral Cartridge
Restores safe pH and adds essential minerals for drinkability.

Sealed Waste Cartridge

All concentrated waste is routed into a separate sealed cartridge. The cartridge is ultrasonically welded and resin‑encapsulated, preventing leaks or user exposure. Users never directly handle hazardous materials.
