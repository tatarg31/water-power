What is Water Power?

Water Power is a modular water filtration system designed to remove biological, chemical, physical, and radiological contaminants while responsibly managing the waste it captures. It makes use of Artificial Intelligence in the expert field as it analyses the data on it's own and gives real-time feedback to the user. Clean water is a basic human necessity and a recognized human right, yet billions of people worldwide still lack reliable access to safe drinking water. According to the United Nations, over two billion people live in water-stressed regions where clean water is scarce or unreliable, according to the United Nations. This lack of access contributes directly to preventable diseases, economic instability, and reduced quality of life. This product was made in response to this issue in hopes of achieveing SDG 6 (Sustainable Development Goals) which is Clean Water and Sanitation.

How It Works

After pouring water into the filter, sensors at the top detect what needs to be cleaned out so the water is drinkable. This reduces wasteful use of filters that aren't always needed to clean the water, therefore extending the filter's life. The sensors, with the help of AI, also give real-time, easy to understand feedback to the user so they can be knowledgeable about the water they drink. Then the water flows through the filters and is then dispensed out of another opening.
Raw Water → Debris Screen → Bio Filter → Carbon → RO → pH Control → Clean Water

What It Removes

  • Bacteria, parasites, protozoa
  • Sand, sediment, insects
  • Heavy metals (lead, mercury)
  • Chemicals & pesticides
  • Microplastics & salts
  • Dissolved radionuclides

Lifecycle & Waste Responsibility

Captured contaminants are sealed inside a dedicated waste cartridge using ultrasonic welding and resin encapsulation.
Spent cartridges are collected by licensed waste processors for regeneration, material recovery, or regulated disposal.

Limitations

Not a replacement for municipal treatment. Requires regular cartridge replacement.
